Location Master App Privacy Policy

Introduction
Thank you for using the Location Master App. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using this app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.
Information We Collect
1. Location Data:
o The app collects precise location data, including latitude, longitude, altitude, accuracy, and address.
o Location data is collected when:
 You search for locations.
 Use the app's features like Points, Paths, or Polygons.
 Enable real-time path or polygon tracking.
o Purpose: This data is used to provide accurate geo-information, calculate distances and areas, and enable features like saving, editing, and exporting geospatial data.
2. Media and Gallery Access:
o The app requires access to your device’s camera and gallery to:
 Capture images with overlaid location details such as latitude, longitude, altitude, address, date, and time.
 Allow you to save and share these images.
o Purpose: This enables the creation of location-tagged photos for your personal use and record-keeping.
How We Use Your Information
The information collected is used to:
• Provide app functionalities such as location tracking, data export, and geospatial calculations.
• Allow the creation and sharing of location-tagged photos.
Permissions Required
1. Location Permission:
o The app requires access to your device’s location services to collect real-time location data.
o This permission is essential for features like location tracking, geospatial calculations, and exporting geo-data.
2. Media and Gallery Permission:
o The app requires access to your camera and gallery to capture and store images with overlaid location details.
Data Sharing: We do not share your data with third parties, nor do we store it on our own servers.
Your Choices
• You can deny location or media permissions, but this may limit the app’s functionality.
• You can delete saved data, including points, paths, polygons, and images, from within the app.
Changes to the Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and we recommend reviewing this page periodically.
